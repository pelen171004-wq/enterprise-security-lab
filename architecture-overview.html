The Enterprise Security Lab is built around a segmented architecture using FortiGate as the central enforcement point and Cisco CBS250 as the Layer 2 access switch.

Current production zones include Users, Management and Guest networks. Planned expansion includes SOC, Servers and Backup zones.

The architecture follows least privilege, management plane isolation and default-deny inter-VLAN control.
