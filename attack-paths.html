Attack Simulation Summary

This simulation represents an untrusted endpoint located in the Guest VLAN attempting to move laterally into protected internal zones. The FortiGate firewall inspects the traffic path and blocks unauthorized access according to the segmentation policy model.

Security Meaning

This model demonstrates how VLAN segmentation and firewall policy enforcement reduce attack surface and prevent lateral movement. It reflects a Zero Trust inspired design where untrusted endpoints are never allowed to directly reach internal or management networks.

Simulated attack paths are animated from the Guest zone toward protected internal networks. Deny policies stop the flow before it reaches Users or Management.
