Role

Cisco CBS250 provides VLAN segmentation and Layer-2 switching for the Enterprise Security Lab network.

VLAN Configuration

  • VLAN20 – Users
  • VLAN30 – Management
  • VLAN60 – Guest

Port Layout

  • Gi1 – trunk to FortiGate
  • Gi2 – management access
  • Gi3 – admin access
  • Gi4 – guest VLAN
  • Gi5 – users VLAN
