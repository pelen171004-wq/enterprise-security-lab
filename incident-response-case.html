Phase 5 — Post-MortemCompleted · T+120 min
Root cause: Unmanaged device on Guest VLAN exhibited scanning behavior consistent with IoT malware (Mirai variant suspected).

What worked: FortiGate policy ID 8 immediately denied all lateral movement attempts. IPS detected and alerted within seconds. Zero VLAN20 hosts were affected.

Improvements identified: Add port security MAC limit on Guest ports (SW-A gi6). Consider captive portal for Guest VLAN. Add IoT VLAN40 as dedicated zone. MTTR: 11 minutes from detection to confirmed containment.
