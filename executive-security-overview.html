Executive Summary:
The current lab demonstrates strong segmentation and effective firewall enforcement. Primary risks relate to untrusted guest behavior, management protection and the need for continued monitoring maturity. Core controls are effective, but expanding telemetry and incident correlation would further improve resilience.
