Overview

This page identifies the main exposure points in the lab and the controls used to reduce risk. The focus is on network entry points, management interfaces and lateral movement paths.

Security Value of Segmentation

Segmentation reduces the blast radius of compromise. Guest systems are treated as untrusted by design, users are limited to required connectivity and management traffic is isolated from general endpoint access.
