This page documents potential attack vectors in the lab environment and the security controls implemented to mitigate them.

Possible Threats

  • Internet scanning
  • Compromised guest device
  • Compromised user workstation
  • Unauthorized management access

Mitigation Strategies

  • Firewall policy enforcement
  • Network segmentation
  • Management VLAN isolation
  • Restricted east-west traffic
